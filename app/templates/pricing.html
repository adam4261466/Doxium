Frequently Asked Questions
What file types does Pro support?

PDF, DOCX, PPTX, XLSX, CSV, TXT, MD, HTML, and JSON. Free plan is PDF only.

Do document upload limits reset monthly?

No — they are lifetime totals. You can upload up to 50 documents total on Pro. AI query limits (100/month) do reset on your billing date.

What happens when I cancel?

Your Pro access continues until the billing period ends, then your account reverts to Free limits. Files beyond the Free limit are kept for 30 days for you to download.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee instead — you get full Pro access immediately and can request a full refund within 7 days if you're not satisfied.

How are queries counted?

Each question submitted in the dashboard counts as 1 query, regardless of how long your documents are. Queries reset on your monthly billing date.
