Frequently Asked Questions

What document formats are supported?

The Free plan supports PDF files. Pro and Enterprise plans support PDF, DOCX, TXT, Markdown, HTML, and more formats.

How does the AI query work?

Upload a document and ask questions in natural language. Doxium indexes your content with FAISS and uses AI to generate accurate answers with source references.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard. Your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. Files are encrypted at rest and in transit. We use isolated storage per user and never share your data with third parties.
