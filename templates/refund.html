Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Summary: Full refund within 7 days of your first Pro payment. Monthly renewals are non-refundable once charged. The Free plan has no charge and is not subject to this policy.

1. Plans Overview

Plan Price Billing Refundable?
Free $0 N/A N/A — no charge
Pro $7 / month Monthly via Lemon Squeezy First payment within 7 days ✓

2. 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you are not satisfied after your first Pro payment, request a full refund within 7 calendar days of the charge date.

To request a refund, email support@doxium.app with:

  • Subject: "Refund Request – Pro"
  • The email address on your account
  • A brief reason (optional)

Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days to your original payment method.

3. Renewal Charges

Monthly renewals are non-refundable once processed. Cancel before your renewal date to avoid the next charge. Your Pro access continues until the end of the current paid period.

4. How to Cancel

  • Via the Lemon Squeezy customer portal (link in your subscription confirmation email)
  • By emailing support@doxium.app — processed within 1 business day

After cancellation your account reverts to the Free plan (3 docs, 10 queries/month, 50 MB, PDF only). Documents beyond Free limits are kept for 30 days before deletion — download them beforehand.

5. Exceptions — When Refunds May Be Declined

  • Violation of our Terms of Service
  • A refund was already issued to the same account within the past 12 months
  • Request received more than 7 days after the first charge

6. Extended Outages

In the event of a continuous service outage exceeding 72 hours caused by Doxium infrastructure, affected Pro subscribers may request a pro-rata credit against their next billing cycle. Email support@doxium.app to request a review.

7. Contact

Billing questions or refund requests: support@doxium.app — we respond within 2 business days.
